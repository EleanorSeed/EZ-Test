Contact Mic Workshop: Making Contact

PINK mcr 10 August 2025 [past]

XMTR festival 26th-28th September 2025 [past]

A workshop, led by Manchester-based sound designer and composer Emily Zurowski, where participants are introduced to the world of contact microphones and given the opportunity to make their own experimental sounds and soundscapes. See below for a video of a recent workshop held at PINK mcr
