Emily Zurowski
Manchester-based Sound Designer & Composer

Emily is a Scottish composer, audio producer and performer based in Manchester. She has worked as a session musician with artists such as The Orielles and WH Lung, touring internationally and performing at festivals like SXSW and Green Man Festival. In the last year, she has co-created immersive installations as part of a Manchester-based collective and composed original soundtracks for documentaries by Maire Tracey. She also hosted the radio show Hypnagogiao on Reform Radio for a number of years. Currently, Emily releases music under the artist name MODEMA and has received praise from Clash Magazine and BBC Radio 6. Her collaborative track with Ecuadorian composer Joaquin Cornejo has accumulated over 9 million streams to date. Emily's current interests lie in community-focused projects and using the arts as a vehicle for social change.
