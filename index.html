Emily Zurowski

Sound Designer | Composer

Emily is a Scottish composer, audio producer and performer based in Manchester. She has worked as a session musician (keys, synth, and vocals) with artists such as The Orielles (2019-2023) and WH Lung, touring internationally and performing at festivals such as SXSW and Green Man Festival. She has released music as an independent artist, receiving praise from Clash Magazine and BBC Radio 6. She has co-created immersive audio-visual installations as part of a Manchester-based creative collective, and composed original soundtracks for two documentaries by award-winning filmmaker Maire Tracy. Between 2021 and 2025, Emily hosted the radio show Hypnagogia on Reform Radio. Her current interests lie in composing original music and producing sound design for visual and interactive media, co-creating work through community-focused projects, and using the arts as a vehicle for social change.
