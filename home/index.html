Emily Zurowski
Manchester-based Sound Designer & Composer

Emily is a Scottish composer, sound designer, and performer based in Manchester. In the past year, she has composed an original soundtrack for a documentary by award-winning documentary maker Maire Tracey and co-created several immersive installations as part of a Manchester-based collective. Emily releases music under the artist name MODEMA.
